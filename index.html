Entre'

This selection of unique entrances offers style and performance with a price point designed specifically to offer exceptional value for money.

The entrance doors comprise many of the same quality components as our Excellence door range and have a flush interior and exterior and now offer U-values of 0.90W/sq.mk. In addition, all Entre doors are Secured by Design Certified, European security level RC2 and RC3 and UK PAS 24 Certified, all of which delivers high levels of protection from unwanted intruders and the experienced burglar.

Pivot

All of our Excellence front doors can be upgraded to a Pivot door, or we can work with your ideas and create a bespoke design just for you.

This prestigious door style is available for all our door designs, both standard and custom, with the added benefit of grand door sizes up to 2000mm wide x 3000mm high*, in a single panel. In addition, sidelights and fanlights are available within a single frame construction to create the ultimate entrance.

These new Pivot doors use our Exclusive profile, manufactured from premium German components and benefit from our hallmark flush door and frame construction system. These doors achieve a U Value of 0.72W/sq.mK and have full PAS 24 and Secure by Design enhanced security certification as standard, offering high levels of protection from the elements and unwanted intruders.
